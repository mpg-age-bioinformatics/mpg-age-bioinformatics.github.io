Infrastructure

Our day is spent on the cluster.

Working tightly with the IT department, and strongly supported by our dedicatd systems administrator we assure that no project is limited by computational power or any other type of IT infrastructure.

Our HPC cluster nodes are supported by a distributed parallel file system (BeeGFS) running on an infiniband network. Nodes have a minimum of 40 processors and 256GB of memory.

We make sure that 80% of jobs get started at their respective maximum performance within 20 minutes of submission and 98% of jobs within 3 hours.

More information on our local HPC cluster can be found here.

Additional HPC resources are available at the Max Planck Computing & Data Facility. Click here for easy porting, software installation instructions, and running pipelines.
