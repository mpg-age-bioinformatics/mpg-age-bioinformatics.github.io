New users

For Apps and submission of analysis requests please use Flaski.
Feel free to contact us if you need more help.
Please mail us if you would like access to the inhouse HPC.
For access to the resources of the Max Planck Computing and Data Facility (MPCDF) you can register here.
Once your registration at the MPCDF is confirmed you can activate further services here.

Why?
By making use of the already existing infrastructure of the Max Planck Society we are decreasing our overheads - hardware costs, electrical bill, human resources. This means we have more time to work on what really matters, your projects.

How?
Our High Performance Computing Cluster is located in-house at the Max Planck Institute for Biology of Ageing in Cologne.
Our public content is hosted at GitHub.com.
Our private content is hosted by a GitHub Enterprise solution at the Max Planck Institute for Molecular Genetics in Berlin.
Our data is delivered and backed up at the Max Planck Computing and Data Facility in Garching.
1. Once you register in Garching we will be asked if you should be granted access. We will confirm your affiliation and you will receive a confirmation email.
2. Once you have activated your cloud storage solution we will associate your user name with your project and you will be automatically notified everytime new data is available.
3. We will then ask Berlin to send you an user account for the GitHub Enterprise solution. This is the account you need to access your projects here.
